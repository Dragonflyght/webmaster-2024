Programs

Clean Home Energy Incentives

Program Benefits Eligibility Learn More
Residential Clean Energy Credit 30% of cost of recently installed clean energy systems in your home, in tax credits Previously used systems and homes that you do not live in are not eligible irs.gov
Residential Renewable Energy Tax Credit Tax credit of up to 30% of the cost of eligible renewable energy systems Eligible systems include solar-electric, solar water heating, fuel cell, small wind energy, geothermal, and battery storage systems dsireusa.org

Energy-Efficient Home Incentives

Program Benefits Eligibility Learn More
Energy-Efficient New Homes Tax Credit for Home Builders Up to $5,000 tax credit per house (or unit in multifamily building) for home builders Home must meet energy efficiency standards as outlined in the IRS Notice 2023-65 dsireusa.org
EnergyStar Federal Tax Credits for Energy Efficiency Up to $3,200 tax credit for up to 30% of the cost of installing energy-efficient systems in your home Home must be your main residence, must be in the U.S., and must not be newly built. energystar.gov
Energy Efficient Mortgages (EEMs) Favorable mortgage terms for qualifying energy-efficient house purchases or upgrades Home energy assessment generally required; varies by mortgage energystar.gov

Electric Vehicle Incentives

Program Benefits Eligibility Learn More
Federal Tax Credits for New Plug-in Electric and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (2023+) 30% of car cost, up to $7,500, in tax credits Vehicle must be new, be purchased in or after 2023, and meet requirements as listed on fueleconomy.gov fueleconomy.gov
Federal Tax Credits for Pre-Owned Plug-in Electric and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (2023+) 30% of car cost, up to $4,000, in tax credits Vehicle must be purchased from a dealer in or after 2023, have a sale price of $25,000 or less, and meet requirements as listed on fueleconomy.gov fueleconomy.gov
Federal Tax Credits for New All-Electric and Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (2010-2023) Up to $7,500 in tax credits Vehicle must be purchased new, be purchased between 2010 and 2023, and meet requirements as listed on fueleconomy.gov fueleconomy.gov
Personal Alternative Fuel Vehicle Refueling Property Tax Credit Tax credit of 30% of the cost of alternative vehicle refueling equipment, including electric vehicle charging equipment, up to $1,000 Equipment must be used to recharge electric vehicles or store/dispose of clean-burning fuel, and must be installed during the current tax year irs.gov
