Our Mission

LiveGreen is a nonprofit web organization dedicated to informing homeowners of the possibilities and assistance available to install clean energy in their homes. We believe that big change starts with the individual, and that your choice to use renewable energy will make an impact in your life, your community, and the world.

Thanks to government programs and advancements in technology, clean energy is becoming more accessible.

Since 2009, the price of solar panels has fallen by

83%

and the price of wind power has fallen by over half.
