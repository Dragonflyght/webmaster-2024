Why go green?

Renewable (also known as clean or green energy) benefits the planet, but it can also benefit you. Not only is it often cheaper in the long run, but there are many government-sponsored initiatives that reward homeowners for the use of green energy. These programs can support you if you choose to install renewable energy systems in your home.
